# 🚀 CrumbRank AI

### AI-Powered Recruitment & Candidate Ranking System

Developed by **Coding_Crumbs**

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Recruiters spend significant time manually screening resumes, often relying on keyword-based Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). These systems may overlook qualified candidates due to limited contextual understanding.

---

## 💡 Solution

No Crumbs Left is an AI-powered recruitment assistant that intelligently ranks candidates using semantic similarity and multiple recruitment signals, helping recruiters make faster and smarter hiring decisions.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 AI Candidate Ranking
- 📊 Interactive Dashboard
- 👤 Candidate Profile Analysis
- 💪 AI Strength & Weakness Assessment
- 📧 Recruitment Email Generator
- 👥 Recruitment Center
- 📈 Candidate Analytics

---
